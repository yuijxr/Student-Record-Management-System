Guides

PushBack: Add new student record at the end/bottom of the list.

PushFront: Add new student record at the beginning/top of the list.

PopBack: Delete student record at the end/bottom of the list.

PopFront: Delete student record at the beginning/top of the list.

Peek: Shows the first/topmost(head) record of the list, it does not remove the record.

isEmpty: Checks if the list is empty, it will return a boolean value(true or false) depending on the result.

Search: Search a student record in the list, It will return the index of the record.
